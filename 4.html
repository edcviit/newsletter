    INTRODUCTION


    Lately, we have been a part of a mega sports event - the Cricket World Cup, 2023. Although none of us were players, we were completely engrossed in the event. It’s better to describe it as an emotional connection. It was fascinating to see how individuals like us could control our emotions through a wooden stick and a leather ball. These cricketers may be labeled as magicians, but at the end of the day, they are ordinary beings—humans, just like us. It is mere emotions that the cricketers portray on the field. This emotion is the nurturing ground for all of us, and as fans, we express our enthusiasm, engage in spirited discussions, shed tears, debate, pray, feel fearful, and find amusement. These emotions, shared by players and sports enthusiasts, make the World Cup truly invaluable.


    UNRAVELING THE CWC 20223 LOGO

This realm of emotion has various ups and downs which helps to reach the pinnacle of success.


    The Entrepreneurial Connect


    Navras being a blend of emotions also helps in building a successful entrepreneurial mindset, now let us see how navaras help to create a landscape of business and lead towards success.


    The Nine Emotions - Navaras


    Ras one: Shringara
    Shringara, the skill of managing emotions, empowers entrepreneurs to foster connections, build relationships, and infuse their endeavors with purpose and responsibility.


    Ras two: Hasya
    Hasya is the emotion of laughter and joy. Using humor effectively can help build positive relationships and create a joyful atmosphere.


    Ras three: Karuna
    Karuna is the deep understanding and compassion that resonates with the emotions and needs of others. When applied effectively, it helps create genuine solutions to address their problems.


    Ras four: Raudra
    Raudra is anger, when controlled it helps a person make firm decisions, set goals, and face challenges head-on.



    Ras five: Veera
    Veera is the emotion of strength and courage. It pushes us to take on challenges, work towards our goals, and develop our understanding.


    Ras six: Bhayanaka
    Being cautious in managing risks is normal. Bhayanaka, the emotion associated with fear, can aid in assessing risks and making informed decisions. It takes courage to face challenges regardless of the outcome.


    Ras seven: Bibhatsya
    Bibhatsya urges us to confront aversion within. Recognize its link to critical evaluation skills. Prioritize your well-being and emotional health as you navigate challenges.


    Ras eight: Adbhuta
    Adbhuta is an emotion that fuels innovation and creativity. It challenges individuals to push beyond conventional thinking and create remarkable things.


    Ras nine: Shanta
    Shanta is the ability to remain composed and resilient in facing challenges. A calm and composed demeanor during turbulent times is linked to this skill.



    CONCLUSION

    Think of the Navaras like a bunch of feelings, but now picture them mixed up with entrepreneurial vibes. Symbols and colors team up to show how people feel all sorts of emotions while enjoying the excitement of building their start-ups. It's like turning emotions into a business adventure!
